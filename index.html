Overview

To learn Javascript,CSS,HTML & a framework JQuery, Vue , Angular, Swift or React For Front End development.

STUDY TIPS

  • Learn with 2:1 Ratio / 2 hours studying - 1 hour hands on
  • Journal what you learned
  • keep track of all your Why's (questions)
  • Adopt coding mindset - anything can be Googled!
  • Always think of solutions to problems no matter how big or small
